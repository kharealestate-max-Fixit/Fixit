Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

⚠️ This is a starting template, not legal advice. Have a professional tailor it to your business and the laws that apply to you (e.g. GDPR, CCPA) before scaling.

1. Information we collect

Account details (name, email, phone), contractor business details and location, job descriptions and photos you upload, messages you send, and booking/payment metadata. Payment card details are collected and processed directly by Stripe — we do not store full card numbers.

2. How we use it

To operate the marketplace: matching homeowners and contractors, enabling chat and notifications, processing payments, displaying reviews, preventing fraud, and improving the service.

3. AI photo analysis

Photos and descriptions you submit for diagnosis are sent to our AI provider (e.g. Google Gemini) to generate a suggested diagnosis and repair options. Do not upload sensitive personal information in photos.

4. Sharing

We share the information needed to complete a booking between the matched homeowner and contractor. We use service providers (hosting, database, payments, push notifications, AI) who process data on our behalf. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Cookies & storage

We use local storage to keep you logged in and to manage push-notification subscriptions.

6. Your choices

You can update your account info, disable push notifications in your browser, and request deletion of your account by emailing us.

7. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and hashed passwords. No method is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

8. Contact

Privacy questions? Email support@fixit18.com.
